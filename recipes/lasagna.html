Lasagna

Description

This isn't your ordinary lasagna. This recipe creates the most perfect, traditional lasagna you've ever laid your eyes on.

Ingredients

  • 15oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all of the ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 375°.
  3. Begin boiling a large pot of salted pasta water for the lasagna noodles. Once a rapid boil is reached, cook the noodles to al dente according to package instructions. (Set a timer to ensure you don’t overcook them.) Gently stir with a wooden spoon throughout cooking to prevent the noodles from sticking. Drain and rinse with cold water until noodles are completely cool.
  4. Lay the cooled noodles flat on wax or parchment paper while you begin assembling the lasagna.
  5. Spread 1 heaping cup of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9 x 13-inch casserole dish. Next, add 4 lasagna noodles, overlapping them if needed.
  6. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by about 1 ½ cups of meat sauce.
  7. Top with 4 more lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture, 1 ½ cups meat sauce.
  8. Finish with 4 more lasagna noodles, the rest of the ricotta mixture, and the rest of the meat sauce.
  9. Top with 2 ½ cups of Mozzarella cheese.
  10. Spray foil with nonstick cooking spray and place it spray-side-down on top of the baking dish to prevent the cheese from sticking to it.
